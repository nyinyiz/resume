Curtain wipe mechanics
Going forward (→ next)
Incoming slide starts at clip-path: inset(0 100% 0 0)
Animates to inset(0 0% 0 0)
Outgoing slide stays put underneath.
Duration: 650ms · Easing: cubic-bezier(0.76, 0, 0.24, 1)
Going backward (← prev)
Incoming slide starts at clip-path: inset(0 0 0 100%)
Animates to inset(0 0 0 0%)
Mirror of forward — wipes from left.
Duration: 650ms · Same easing
